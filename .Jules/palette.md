## 2025-05-14 - Interactive Grid Accessibility and Feedback
**Learning:** In dark-themed applications, interactive grid elements (like attendance toggles) benefit significantly from 'brightness-125' hover states and explicit focus rings to differentiate from the background and provide clear state feedback.
**Action:** Always provide descriptive `aria-label` and `title` for grid buttons that include the context (e.g., student name, date, and current status) to ensure accessibility for screen reader users.
