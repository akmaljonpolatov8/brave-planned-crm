## 2024-05-20 - [Accessibility Enhancement: ARIA Labels & Titles]
**Learning:** Adding descriptive ARIA labels to interactive elements, especially icon-only buttons and complex grids like the attendance calendar, significantly improves the experience for screen reader users and provides better context via hover titles.
**Action:** Always ensure icon-only buttons have an `aria-label` and complex interactive grid cells have both `aria-label` and `title` that include contextual information (e.g., entity name, date, current status).
